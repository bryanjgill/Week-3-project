The FX is now the QX

The Infiniti FX, the Flagship of the Infiniti brand has received a promotion. The FX line is now part of the QX line. The FX35, FX45 and FX50 will now be designated as the "QX70 3.7", "QX 3.7 AWD" and "QX70 5.0 AWD" respectively. With this new designation, the entire Infiniti fleet has been consolidated into four models, the G, M, Q and QX. Please visit the Infinity website for more information on the G, M and Q models.

The QX70 comes with a 3.7-liter V-6 or optional 5.0-liter V-8 engine, Continuous Variable Transmission, 360° camera view for safety and optional All-Wheel Drive.
